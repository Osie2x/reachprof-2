# Voice Samples

Paste 3-5 real past emails here (from your sent folder). Redact names and
identifying details as needed, but preserve sentence rhythm and word choice.
These samples are the single biggest lever on whether generated emails sound
like you.

## sample 1
Hey Prof [X], I came across your recent paper on [topic] and had a quick question about [specific method]. I'm working on something similar this term and got stuck on [problem]. If you had 15 minutes in the next couple weeks, I'd really appreciate any pointers. Otherwise, totally fine. Thanks, Osie.
